Happy hour in Little Italy isn't hard to find. Nearly every restaurant along India Street runs some version of a discounted afternoon window. What's harder to find is a happy hour worth planning around, one with a real setting, a drink list that doesn't feel like an afterthought, and a time slot that actually fits into a weekday.

## What to Look For

Not all happy hours are built the same. A few things separate the ones worth seeking out:

**Daily availability.** A lot of happy hour programs only run two or three days a week, which makes them hard to build a routine around. A daily window is far more useful if happy hour is something you actually want to make a habit of.

**A real drink program, not just well liquor.** The best happy hours discount the same craft cocktails you'd order at full price, not a separate, lesser list.

**A setting that makes the hour feel like a destination**, not just a discount. This is where a patio or a distinctive bar setup matters. An hour spent in a generic dining room doesn't hit the same as one spent somewhere with a view or genuine atmosphere.

## Coco Maya's Happy Hour

Coco Maya runs happy hour daily from 3:00 to 5:00 PM, on the same elevated patio and glass atrium bar used for full dinner service. The two golden hours in the middle of the afternoon are timed for exactly the kind of low-key, mid-day drink that Little Italy's climate is built for, before the dinner rush, while the patio still has good light.

What makes it worth the trip:

- **Daily, not day-of-week specific.** Monday through Sunday, the window holds steady, which makes it far easier to plan around.
- **Discounted cocktails, wine, and select bites**, not a stripped-down separate menu.
- **The same elevated patio and bar area used for full dinner service**, so the setting doesn't feel like a consolation compared to a regular reservation.

## How to Plan Around It

If you work near Little Italy or are exploring the neighborhood in the afternoon, the 3 to 5 PM window is an easy way to experience Coco Maya's patio and cocktail program without committing to a full dinner reservation. It also pairs naturally with an early dinner elsewhere in the neighborhood, or a longer evening that starts with happy hour and rolls straight into dinner on-site.

Because the window is daily, it's one of the more flexible ways to get a table without needing to book far in advance, particularly on weekdays.

## The Bottom Line

Little Italy has no shortage of afternoon drink specials, but the ones worth seeking out are daily, serve real cocktails at a discount, and put you somewhere worth being. Coco Maya's 3 to 5 PM happy hour checks all three boxes, on the same elevated patio that anchors the rest of the restaurant.
